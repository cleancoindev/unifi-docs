Function: earnByPump

earnByPump(address stableCoinAddress, uint256 pairIndex, uint256 pairAmount, uint256 amountAMin, uint256 amountBMin, address tokenAddress, uint256 tokenValue)

Description

Earn by pumping $uSD: Swap stablecoins for $uSD in their pools, then burn $uSD until equilibrium.

Parameters:

  • stableCoinAddress: Address of the $uSD stablecoin

  • pairIndex: Index of the pair inside the whitelisted pairs array

  • pairAmount: Amount of Uniswap liquidity tokens to send back to the pool

  • amountAMin: The minimum amount of tokenA that must be received for the transaction not to revert

  • amountBMin: The minimum amount of tokenB that must be received for the transaction not to revert

  • tokenAddress: Address of the token to swap to get $uSD

  • tokenValue: How much to swap for

Function: earnByDump

earnByDump(address stableCoinAddress, uint256 pairIndex, uint256 amountA, uint256 amountB, uint256 amountAMin, uint256 amountBMin, uint256[] tokenIndices, uint256[] stableCoinAmounts)

Description

Earn by dumping $uSD: Mint stablecoins obtaining $usd then swap it back for caller choice of stablecoins

Parameters:

  • stableCoinAddress: Address of the uSD stablecoin

  • pairIndex: Index of the pair inside the whitelisted pairs array

  • amountA: Amount of tokenA

  • amountB: Amount of tokenB

  • amountAMin: The minimum amount of tokenA that must be received for the transaction not to revert

  • amountBMin: The minimum amount of tokenB that must be received for the transaction not to revert

  • tokenIndices: Array of indices identifying which stablecoins to obtain as result of the arbitrage

  • stableCoinAmounts: Array of containing the amounts of stablecoins to get as result of the arbitrage
